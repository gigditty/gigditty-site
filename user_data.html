Account & Data Deletion

Effective Date: 04/03/2025

GigDitty values your privacy and provides simple ways to delete your account and all associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

You can request account and data deletion directly from within the GigDitty app:

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to the Profile tab
  3. Tap Delete Account

This will permanently delete your account and all related data from our systems.

Alternatively

If you are unable to access the app or prefer a manual request, you can email us at:

support@gigditty.com
